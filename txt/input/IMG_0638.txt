M: It is one of the most famous portraits ever made. Some say it is the most copied image in history. It was on the cover of LIFE magazine when WWII ended. The photo was taken by Yousuf Karsh, one of the world’s most famous portrait photographers, on December 30th, 1941. Churchill angrily told Karsh, “You have two minutes. And that's it, two minutes.” The truth was that Churchill was angry that he had not been told he was to be photographed; he lit a fresh cigar and puffed. Karsh
