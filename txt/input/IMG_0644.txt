W: Please join us for the 29th Annual Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival. The 29th Annual Ithaca, New York Apple Harvest Festival will be held from September 30 to October 2. The Apple Harvest attractions such as the apple pie-eating contest and the farmers' market will return again this year. In addition to the traditional Apple Harvest activities, this year's festival will feature a variety of entertainment, including music and dance. To get into the Apple Harvest Festival, it is free. Over the course of the three-day, event, fresh-farm produce and home-baked goods will be available to taste and buy. Local people will be there to provide the finest locally produced drinks. Come and join us for fun and excitement.
